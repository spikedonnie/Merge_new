Customization

I have added a healthbar prefab with two colors but you can customize it or make your own and use it!.

Its really easy to do!

1) Create a slider on any canvas.

2) Add Healthbar Controller component to it and drag the slider into fields.

3) Customize it to your liking and save it as a prefab.

4) Drag and drop it to healthbar field of 'Easy Multiple Healthbar' prefab in prefabs folder.

Now it will use your own healthbar visual and work the same
